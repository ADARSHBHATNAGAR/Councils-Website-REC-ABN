Cultural Council

About Us

We, at The Cultural Council of Rajkiya Engineering College, Amebdkar Nagar, fill the non-academic calendar with zest and colour by organising intra-college cultural fests and events. We take upon us the responsibility to give the students opportunities to express and explore their talents and have some fun amidst the highly demanding academic life at the college. It is our mission to keep the institute's culture up and intact and fill the college life of students with unforgettable memories.

Events

The Cultural Council has organised numerous unforgettable events in the past. We have dazzled fresh faces on Fresher's Night, made people groove to the beats of traditional sounds in cultural nights, and turned the dial of mystique on Masquerade. Diwali Celebrations, Halloween Party, Retro Night, and many more events are a part of our college's rich cultural history.

Initiatives

Driven by the urge to enhance the cultural experience of students of Rajkiya Engineering College Ambedkar Nagar, the Cultural Council has taken new initiatives this year.
